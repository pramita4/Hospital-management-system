About

Established in 2005, our hospital has been providing excellent health care services to our patients in all the departments like cardiology, oncology and neurology. We have world class doctors having years of expertise providing excellent treatments to all our patients.

We also provide financial aid to the needy and we are affiliated to WeCareForYou NGO since 2011, which helps the cancer patients avail our treatment free of cost.

At the same time we place a lot of importance on the traditional values of hospitality and compassionate patient care. Our primary concern is to ensure that your health and comfort receives special attention and that you are given the best possible care once you enter the portals of our hospital.
